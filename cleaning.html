Purge and Hygiene

Purge and Hygiene, a project in CamFam that deals with disinfecting, fumigating, cleaning. The project core objective is to ensure that the building interior is safe for human interactions and any illnesses that may result from the air in the space of the building is eliminated.

We Offer

Disinfecting

We have accurate and relevant equipment that kills germs on surfaces and objects which lowers the risk of spreading infection is used.

Screening and Sanitizing

We screen and sanitize teachers, learners, guests and other workers. Our team is responsible for taking temperatures at entrance points and handles daily sanitizing of objects.

Fumigating

We provide eco-safe and odourless extermination services using advanced techniques. Our pest control tasks include cockroach extermination, flies and bugs.

Cleaning

Our cleaning services include sweeping, mopping and vacuuming in order to remove germs, dirt and impurities from surfaces and objects.

Safety Packages

We provide our clients with safety packages that include a local handmade washable and reusable mask, 70% alcohol sanitiser (500ml/1l) and/or wipes.

Combined Services

Our combined services include two or more of the services offered at a discounted rate.
